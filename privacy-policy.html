Flora Focus Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Flora Focus collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Flora Focus app and related services.

Flora Focus is a task and habit app with social and family features. By using Flora Focus, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: username, email address, and password credentials. Passwords are stored in hashed form, not in plain text.
  • Profile information: profile picture URL and any profile details you choose to add or update.
  • Task and app content: tasks, task descriptions, categories, priorities, deadlines, completion status, and related productivity data.
  • Social and family data: friend requests, friend connections, family group memberships, leader/member roles, and family task assignments.
  • Technical and diagnostic data: basic server logs and operational information, such as IP address, request metadata, crash information, and service diagnostics generated when you use the app.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • provide the core task, garden, friends, and family features;
  • save your progress and sync data across supported devices;
  • display your profile, your garden, and social features inside the app;
  • maintain security, prevent abuse, and investigate technical issues;
  • improve app performance, reliability, and product quality.

3. Payments and Premium Features

Flora Focus may offer premium features in the future, including paid family functionality. The current demonstration version does not process in-app payments.

If paid subscriptions are added later, this Privacy Policy may be updated to describe the relevant billing or subscription data flows.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in the following limited cases:

  • Service providers: with infrastructure and hosting providers that help operate Flora Focus, such as backend hosting, database, storage, logging, and app distribution providers.
  • Social features: with other Flora Focus users when required for the app to function, for example when you add friends, join a family group, or allow others to view your garden.
  • Legal or safety reasons: if required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the service.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Account, profile, task, and social data are stored on hosted backend infrastructure so the service can function across devices.

We keep your information for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

When you delete your account through the app, Flora Focus is designed to remove your account data from the application database, subject to temporary copies in backups, logs, or security records that may be retained for a limited time.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • update your username and profile picture from the app settings;
  • delete your account from the app settings;
  • choose whether to use optional social and family features.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and Flora Focus cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

Flora Focus is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it if appropriate.

9. International Processing

Depending on the hosting and infrastructure providers used to operate Flora Focus, your information may be processed or stored in countries other than your own.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the effective date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice inside the app or through another suitable channel.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or privacy-related requests, contact: valadaresleonor@netcabo.pt
